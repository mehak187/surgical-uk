about us

18 years of expertise and continuous learning to give you the best of quality and design.

Maju Asiamas Sdn Bhd was established on 08 April 2013 to provide the services such as designing, fabrication and installation of Engineering plastics, Industrial fiberglass and stainless steel works. This company was renamed from the previously known Poly Fabricate Enterprise which was established in September 1996. We specialize in these fields since 1996 resulting in 18 years of expertise and continuous learning to give you the best of quality and design.

We provide services such as fabrication and production of custom required material and design. We also bring you the best workmanship in piping system, Poly-Propylene materials, PVC tanks, Scrubber units, and such other products. Apart from these products we also have been designing and manufacturing General purpose fume chambers, Ductless fume chambers and also General Poly-Propylene fume chambers. We also provide other services such as custom designing lab cabinetry and furniture of the best quality.

quality policy and objectives

Quality is important to our business because we value our customers. We strive to provide our customers with products and services which meet and even exceed their expectations. We are committed to continuous improvement and have established a Quality Management System which provides a framework for measuring and improving our performance.

We have the following systems and procedures in place to support us in our aim of total customer satisfaction and continuous improvement throughout our business:

  1. 1Regular gathering and monitoring of customer feedback
  2. 2A customer complaints procedure
  3. 3Selection and performance monitoring of suppliers against set criteria
  4. 4Training and development for our employees
  5. 5Regular audit of our internal processes
  6. 6Measurable quality objectives which reflect our business aims
  7. 7Management reviews of audit results, customer feedback and complaints

Our internal procedures are reviewed regularly and are held in a Quality Manual which is madeavailable to employees upon request.

Company Strategy

1. VISION

Become number one in the market we serve and revolutionize this company to have the strengths of a big company combined with the leanness and agility of a small company

2. MISSION

Our mission is to be the leading innovator, developer and provider of general laboratory products, fume exhaust systems, and services. As a team, we will achieve aggressive growth and fair return for our shareholders. We will accomplish this by exceeding the expectations of our customers while conserving resources and preserving the quality of the environment.

3. VALUES

In order to best serve our customers and remain true to our employees, Maju Asiamas Sdn. Bhd. embraces the following core values:

QUALITY

At Maju Asiamas Sdn. Bhd., it denotes a commitment to excellence and superior outcomes, and is reflected in our products, services, employees, and business practices.

INTEGRITY

At Maju Asiamas Sdn. Bhd., we say what we mean and do what we say. We act with unconditional honesty, respect, and courtesy at all times.

ACCOUNTABILITY

We believe in accepting responsibility and the consequences, and taking ownership while embracing common goals, teamwork, and collaborative decision-making.

TEAM WORK

We work in a collaborative environment where team success takes precedence over individual achievement and where we encourage new ideas and practices without judgment or criticism. Our success will come from focused strategic growth with a team and strategic partners that share our values.

CONTINUOUS IMPROVEMENT

We are relentless in our pursuit to creatively, intelligently, and systematically improve in everything we do. From our business practices and technology, to our commitment to personal and professional employee development.
