Specialised BioSafety Solutions Tailored to your Organization

ERLA Technologies (M) Sdn Bhd was founded in 1995 in Malaysia by a team of experts who has a combined experience of more than 50 years in the Biosafety and Clean Air Technology. ERLA was initially formed to service the Scientific community in the Biosafety Research However, due to overwhelming demand for local products. ERLA ventured into, manufacturing our own brand of products which meets Various International Standards.

ONE STOP BIO-CONTAINMENT & BIO-SAFETY SOLUTIONS

ERLA has solid experience in the feasibility and master planning of large projects as well as the design and fabrication of mechanical, electrical, structural and HVAC systems and subsequent installation. ERLA’s architects, engineers and designers are capable of handling traditional biological and chemical analytical laboratories right up to P3 level facilities. Intelligent laboratories are the forte of ERLA and such electronically sophisticated facilities such as the MKAK, Sungai Buloh project is a case in point. ERLA success in Malaysia and abroad at building laboratories and bio-safety systems further indicate the high professional standards to which ERLA adheres to. ERLA's engineers, architectural designers, and staffs are highly trained, skilled and experienced. Due to the ever increasing threat of viruses and disease, ERLA has committed itself to providing its clients with the highest quality of workmanship in the construction of facilities that meet the highest and most stringent international standards.
